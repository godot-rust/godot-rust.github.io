    // Provided method

Expand description

Trait to enable conversions of types from the Variant type.

Required Methods§

fn try_from_variant(variant: &Variant) -> Result<Self, VariantConversionError>

Tries to convert a Variant to Self, allowing to check the success or failure.

Provided Methods§

fn from_variant(variant: &Variant) -> Self

⚠️ Converts from Variant to Self, panicking on error.

This method should generally not be overridden by trait impls, even if conversions are infallible. Implementing Self::try_from_variant suffices.
